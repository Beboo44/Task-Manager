## ?? Tips & Best Practices

### For Users:
1. ? **Use Quick Buttons First** - Fastest way to set deadlines
2. ? **Adjust After** - Fine-tune using calendar picker
3. ? **Round Times** - Use :00, :15, :30, :45 for clean times
4. ? **Check Preview** - Verify deadline before submitting

### For Developers:
1. ? **Always Round** - Server-side validation critical
2. ? **Test Timezones** - Ensure correct behavior across zones
3. ? **Log Deadlines** - Track for debugging
4. ? **Validate Range** - Prevent past deadlines
